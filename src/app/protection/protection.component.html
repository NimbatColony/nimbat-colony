Flight Protection

Flight Protection is tasked with defending and hiding the Colony. They have powerful illusionists, and train some Nimbats to be surprisingly adept fighters, capable of felling beasts much larger than they are. Additionally, when an Advocate returns to the Colony with a new Visitant, Flight Protection is the first to meet with them, to get an understanding as to how they are as a person in order to ensure that they are not dangerous. In some cases, Flight Protection has had some of its members leave the Colony border in order to come to the rescue of an unfortunate passerby under attack.

The Three Sects of Flight Protection

Illusion

Defense

Security

As mentioned above, the illusionist Nimbats are primarily in charge of maintaining the illusory walls that hide the Colony from the public eye. It is impossible to see from the outside. It is impossible to smell, hear, or taste anything from the Colony when one is outside the illusory wall. It’s not even easy to just waltz into the Colony on accident, as several extra illusions are put in place to detract and dissuade travelers and wanderers from stumbling into it. In those rare occasions when someone does arrive, they are deemed “lucky,” and are worthy of investigation. The illusionists also hide other things within the Colony proper.
In circumstances where violence is witnessed, the Defense sect is usually ready to deal with the threat, utilizing their magical skills in a barrage to subdue or outright eliminate the threat. It is said that when situations are dire, the more pronounced and powerful Nimbats in this sect are brought out to individually handle the situation. The resulting conflict might cause damage to the external parts of the Colony, so priority is given to the rest of the sect to get everyone not engaged in the scuffle out of the area.
The Security sect takes on a similar role to that of Defense, but instead it focuses on domestic matters. Clearly-seen bands that are wrapped around the root of the right arm indicate members of this sect. These sorts can be approached when issues arrive, and assistance is needed, whether it’s something violent or there’s just someone in need of help.

Eislyn, Flight Protection Elder
"Eislyn is apparently a master illusionist. So much so that even the inhabitants are not completely aware how much of the environment is fabricated. The only guaranteed illusions she deploys is a majority of the external field that hides the Colony, and the spectral clones that occasionally spawn in public spaces. She is described as a quiet, terse, but overall nice Nimbat. Some say that she is fond of sappy and romantic things, and horror stories as well. When she tells a tale, she deploys her illusions just to spice up the scenery. How terrifying! It’s a very good thing she loves the Colony she protects. I didn’t expect an illusionist to not be a trickster…"
