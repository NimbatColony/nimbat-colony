Visitants
There are other non-Nimbats that have integrated into Colony society entirely. Visitants span the gender spectrum, and the interviewed Visitants have been about the Colony for a wide array of time. Some have merely visited and left intermittently, while others have resided there for a much longer time. It seems that a strong pillar of Nimbat ideology is the introduction and potential incorporation of others into the Colony. When a Nimbat goes out into the world and finds someone that catches their interests, they may escort them back to the Colony itself.
“There exists a kind of nomenclature for the variety of individuals that visit the Colony space, and it comes in the form of a categorizing nomenclature. There are Advocates, and there are Visitants. It seems I take on the mantle of Visitant, just like those before me, stepping past the Colony boundaries and mingling with its makeup. The Nimbat who brought me here, a certain ██████, is thereby the Advocate.”
Upon arrival, the Advocate escorts the Visitant to at least one Council member to introduce them personally while the rest are introduced by secondary means. Many Visitants come only for a few days, and choose to leave. However, there are a few that choose to stay. While Visitants generally have some free reign in the Colony, their open status hinges on their capability to actually aid the goings-on within. While even the least magically-inclined Nimbat can be quite resourceful, the insight (or sometimes the physical advantages) from individuals outside of the Colony can wind up being highly appreciated for different tasks. Intercultural mingling is also encouraged, as it is not uncommon to find Visitants and Nimbats fraternizing. It is not unheard of for Visitants to have taken up residence within the Colony, either.
