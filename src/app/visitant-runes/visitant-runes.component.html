Visitant Runes

Newcomer

The default design for new breeders.
The "wings" are very similar to the geographical shape of the Colony.

Avid

The Colony shape is outlined with a nice sheen of silver.

Peon

The silver sheen evolves to gold.

Loyal

The borders of the entire rune are given a thick outline of a contrasting color,
which is accompanied by “sparkle” markings around the rune.

Devoted

The rune grows substantially, with spark-like markings emanating from the “wings” of the rune.
They reach up to the waist for females, and wrap around the male’s genitalia.

Oathsworn

A very faint glow casts from the rune.
The color of the glow seems to be directly related to your Advocate, as if they choose the color it would eventually become.

Kin

At night your mark produces a light that illuminates your entire loins, and at day it sparkles with the sun’s rays.
