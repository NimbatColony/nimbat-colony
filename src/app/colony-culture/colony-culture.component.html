Colony Culture and Society
Nimbat culture could be considered to be closer to primitive standards by modern understandings, though anthropologists would take quick note of its egalitarian nature similar to the world’s first farmers. Each individual has equal access to the means of survival, but work with emphasis toward their community in order to ascertain that the Colony’s needs are also met. Whether it be learning new magic, cultivating food, or running errands outside of the Colony, they all work towards preservation and general community thriving. In a way, they are not too unlike bees. There are workers, protectors, and a Council that works akin to the Colony’s queen, although the authority is spread out into a representative of each of six Flights rather than one centralized leader.

The Colony itself is matriarchal. At the helm of the entire operation sit six elder Nimbats, each of which lead their own Flight. The Council does not represent a unifying authority body, but it does allow each Flight to coordinate at the deepest level in order to make executive decisions for the Colony as needed. Otherwise, the Council keeps a sense of direction for each Flight, and maintains the operation at large.

Flights
The Colony's citizens are organized loosely into six different Flights, all of which deal with a certain aspect of Colony society. They are as follows:
Flight Proliferation focuses on the continuation of the Nimbat species as a whole, through prenatal care and general health maintenance.
Flight Protection is concerned with keeping the Colony in safe hands from both external threats and internal challenges.
Flight Preservation aims to keep balance in the resources the Colony uses, the life of the forest around them, and the history of the Colony itself.
Flight Populace makes sure every Colony citizen has the services they need to learn and grow.
Flight Progression takes information from the outside world, and works to incorporate it into Colony society.
Flight Productionensures the colony runs smoothly by seeing that the proper amount of material goods are provided to everyone. Additionally, there is a small number of Nimbats who choose to exist outside of the traditional Flight structure. They are referred to as "Flightless", however it is understood that is not any sort of insult. Most everyone understands the idea of not wanting to belong to any one Flight, and it is mostly seen as a time for a Nimbat to discover which they would like to end up becoming a part of, if any.
