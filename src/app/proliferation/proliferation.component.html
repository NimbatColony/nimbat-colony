Flight Proliferation

Flight Proliferation is all about the continuation of the Nimbat species. They’re the breeding experts, and tend to handle the most Visitants. They also handle the majority of child care assistance, and offer incentives to those willing to partake. Visitants that engage in this, for example, are given housing and access to the food supply. While Visitants are allowed to have their own jurisdiction within the Colony, they are often encouraged to become breeders. In short, the Proliferation Flight cares about reproducing… a LOT.

The Three Sects of Flight Proliferation

Copulation

Fertility

Midwifing

Nimbats in this sect are more concerned with the act of proliferation within itself. A lot of them take on the task of “bed-warmers,” though, they’ve taken up other tasks as well. Partaking in breeding does not necessarily seem to equivocate to being a part of Flight Proliferation, but those that are a part of Flight Proliferation can partake in breeding. In some instances, these Nimbats just might observe, or even become a part of the action on request.
These Nimbats aren’t often part of the act itself, but play an integral part in ensuring copulation. Whether by magic or apothecary means, they provide partners with supplements that provide effects such as ovulation, increased sperm production, increased potency and virility, and even more exciting measures such as anatomical manipulation.
When it comes to childbirth and early-life care, the Nimbats of the Midwifing sect are experts. When Visitants became more of a regular occurrence, adaptation had to be done, but now Nimbat and Visitant alike are referred to Nimbats of Flight Populace once the family is off to their own devices.

Rosmerta, Flight Proliferation Elder
"Rosmerta was a… fascinating meeting, to say the least. If I were to openly describe what sort of Nimbat she is, I’d say she is awfully motherly. In hindsight, I suppose that’s to be expected. Her magic seems to focus on fertility aspects, such as increasing stimulation through mere touch, among…other effects. Her entire demeanor isn’t completely focused on her Flight’s namesake, though. Rosmerta has said that she is into a few of the local sports in the Colony, such as a kind of activity that I can only describe as some kind of aerial volleyball. Others in the Colony have warned me not to cross those in her care. Apparently, underneath her honey-sweet exterior, is a fantastic display of power. Her true magic potential offers up a whole barrage of elemental power. Mothers have to be tough, I suppose."
