Is involvement with the Colony Chronicles just between the elders and anons or do nimbats within the community get their time to shine? If so, does that apply to patreon nims only or is everyone fair game? -Anon

As for right now, Chronicles are stories that happen between non-player characters, whether that be Elders, Sect Leaders, other Nimbats in the colony, or anonymous/non-anonymous, non-player Visitants. They are, at large, supposed to be looks into every-day life in the Colony, to give members of the community a bit of a look into what I guess could be considered canon.

A note on that: The Nimbat Colony setting is to be used more or less how you, as the player/writer, would like it to be. Colony Chronicles are, in a way, a way to provide you inspiration for your own stories or roleplays.

We're bouncing ideas around the dev group as to how we may incorporate player character cameos in future stories. We're thinking it may become a monthly raffle or suggestion bin for Patrons of a certain tier, but we're looking at other options as well. I can't really speak to much of the specifics of that plan yet, though!

Hope that helps! If you've got more questions, feel free to @ us on Twitter, or on Discord.

Will there be community events? Like roleplay stuff going on in the room/discord? Any out of roleplay stuff like a movie/game night? Will there be community game servers? If so will any of that be relegated to the patreon supporters? -Anon

There are plans for those in the future, yes! They'd likely be outside of the realm of roleplay, so things like movie or game nights for sure.
