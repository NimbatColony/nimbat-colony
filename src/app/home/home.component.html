The Nimbat Colony
Primarily from the notes of Nimbat researcher █████████, this site is an expository overview of the detailing on the Nimbat species’ physiology, their norms, and the Colony they reside in. There are plenty of portions of the researcher’s field book that are relatively easy to read and decipher, though many more are currently illegible. A team of archivists are working to restore the pages, and the ones that are currently readable are archived here. As more pages are transcribed, and more research is conducted, more will be added to this archive. Throughout the reports, the researcher leaves a few personal notes, detailing their specific interactions with certain parts of the Colony itself.
Further information on lore development and usage can be found on the Logos and Lore Usage Guidelines page.
