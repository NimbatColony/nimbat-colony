Flight Production

Flight Production is focused entirely on making sure the colony has the materials they need to continue on. Whether it be food or tools, the nimbats in Production are the craftsbattes, miners, loggers, and farmers that keep the whole colony moving together.

The Three Sects of Flight Production

Agriculture

Metallurgy

Handicraft

This sect focuses almost entirely on the production of crops. Whether it be for food or materials like cotton, the farmers and gatherers of Agriculture are the lifeblood of the Colony. Crops are inspected, and those working on the production and maintenance of these crops are compensated accordingly. There are also systems proposed and practiced by this sect in order to protect food security in moments of disaster or destruction.
A majority of the workers in Metallurgy are focused on extracting and refining material from the Colony’s mine, as well as those more interested in processing metals for tools and other uses around the Colony. What’s most interesting is that while the mine itself isn’t very rich in ore, the Nimbats that deploy their magics for alchemy are able to transmute stone and other procured items from the mines into potential ore. From there, smelters and forgers are able to process that metal into usable material. They work closely with Preservation’s Maintenance sect, providing them with material that may be needed to retain infrastructural integrity for buildings and other projects.
Handicraft is home to the makers, the movers, the shakers, and the improvers that actually produce the things made from raw material found in its sister sects. Carpenters, weavers, goldsmiths, leatherworkers, blacksmiths and more find themselves at-home in this aspect, crafting any manner of goods that may be needed. Some are even open to teaching interested Nimbats and Visitants, and help them improve their handiwork.
