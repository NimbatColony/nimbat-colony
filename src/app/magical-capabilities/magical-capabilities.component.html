Magical Capabilities
Every Nimbat has innate magical capabilities and a connection to the arcane, but how that capability manifests varies wildly from Nimbat to Nimbat. They report that their magic can take any form, and it is up to the Nimbat themselves to determine how they would like theirs to manifest. They describe it as a sort of “focusing.” They choose to learn about and improve upon the arcane aspects that interest them the most and are within reach, usually as they pertain to their personal hobbies and interests. Through training and understanding, a Nimbat can craft and weave together different schools of magic that exemplify their ability and expression. For example, Nimbats can be powerful illusionists—a large network of them hides the colony from the outside world. To become one, all a Nimbat would have to do is study it, train in it, and their magical focus slowly begins to change. It’s very possible for Nimbats to learn multiple schools of magic rather than just settling for one, but it is not highly-sought after. It is sometimes compared to learning a new language with different rules than the root.
It is perfectly possible for Nimbats to forgo magic altogether, although these examples have been observed to be exceedingly rare.
Recorded observation of multiple Nimbat specimens led to the following list of known Nimbat magic. It should be noted that this is not an exhaustive list, as it seems the Nimbat species will more often “riff” on magics to make them more individualistic.

General Nimbat Magics
The below listed magic tends to be seen as a "universal", as Nimbats more commonly learn these magics.

Elemental Manipulation

Cryomancy

Control over solidified water particles, or ice. The number of capable cryomancers within the Colony are somewhat scarce, but those that hold the various lessons of this chapter of magic make the claim that it’s useful in better controlling Winter conditions and lessening the season’s punishment on crops.
“I have taken to engaging with the gossip in the various arcane student gatherings. Those that often studied the art of cryomancy had a tendency to share myth and rumor regarding an older civilization of Nimbats in the Northern reaches, within the snowy mountains. My colleagues cannot make the claim that they’ve heard of similar reports elsewhere. I may have to inquire with Colony historical scholars to try and find any sort of validity within these stories.”

Pyromancy

Fire conjuration and control. Used mostly for cooking, but sometimes used for defensive purposes, or controlled burns to prevent larger forest fires.
“There is a common idea in the complex of magical teaching: it all began with fire. Not unlike man, really. But unlike we, they abandoned the stone and immediately sought to control what they believed was nature itself. Nature obliged. The end result? The idea of having to set up an entire campfire just to make s’mores tends to make Colony inhabitants marvel at our efforts just to recreate their simplest of spells.”

Bodily Manipulation

Autoamelioration

Alteration of their bodies ability to stretch and accommodate larger than typically expected objects.
“I cannot help but wonder what provoked Nimbats to develop the school of auto-amelioration. Perhaps persistent comments about their smaller stature created a number of spiteful developments. I will, however, refrain from any outward comments on how some Nimbats use these spells to fill up their cheeks with snacks like voracious chipmunks. In case these writings fall into some mischievous paws: Ils ont l'air adorables. J'espère qu'ils ne comprennent pas le français.”

Smokeform

Nimbats with this ability are able to change their entire form into a cloud of smoke. This form is able to move freely, doesn’t seem to be affected by air movement, and allows the Nimbat to reform without any apparent harm to their bodies.
“At some point, even the Nimbats became inspired by the stories and traditions of Samhain. In the Colony, ghosts are a very real presence, and they will begin rearing their spectral, felid faces upon the ives of October. Of course, when I mean “ghosts,” I am implicitly referencing the Nimbats that have taken it upon themselves to learn smokeform enchantments and conveniently bypass the very concept of doorways. On a related note, privacy laws are much more pronounced here.”

Extrasensory

Clairvoyance

Detecting the location of objects they previously had no knowledge of. This tends to be short-ranged. For example, knowing what you have in your bag, or what is in a certain cabinet without looking in it.
“Airport security detail be damned. If you so far as carry the idea of a cupcake in your pocket, you will be frisked from head to toe before you are allowed to see any Elder.”

Telepathic Communication

Some Nimbats are able to communicate with others despite physical means typically disallowing it, such as without actually speaking or across solid surfaces.
“There are a handful of Colony inhabitants that choose to communicate solely through telepathic mediums. In some cases, it is a useful spell reserved for the mute. But I am left to wonder…how am I able to perceive their voice if they do not know what it is?”

Psionic Communication

A choice few telepathic Nimbats are able to not only communicate words, but feelings and experiences. This could potentially include being brought into what seems like a world in their mind.
“The development of human language trends toward a need of efficacy through terseness. Shorter words to convey a complex series of ideas. Even forgoing words altogether in favor of isolated symbols. The empaths here, however, have wrested some certainties that our way is the correct direction.”

"Flight-Specific" Magic
While use of this magic is not explicitly restricted to these specific flights, users of this magic tend to align themselves with the Flights listed in the record.

Elemental Manipulation

Electrokinesis

The control of the flow of electricity. This tends to be rarer, and relatively exclusive to Flight Progression. The Colony tends to use magical sources to produce things like light and heat rather than mechanical or fuel-based sources.
“For a time, electricity bore a very different meaning within the Colony. Electromancers simply believed in the raw power of nature’s most voluminous violence, and put forth that it was a demonstration of might. Simply hearing about how power lines worked quickly turned these students of electromancy into a part of the choir that sang the Progression Flight into existence.”

Phytomancy

The control over plant-based growth. This study of magic is considered particularly useful, as the majority of the Colony is in the canopy of the forest. Phytomancers working in Flight Populace use it to form new dwellings out of the trees already there. It’s quite interesting, and it allows them to create new homes in the span of a few hours at most. On top of this, there are the obvious agricultural benefits.
"Nimbat farmers tend to be phytomancers, but it is not the hard-and-fastened rule that others might think it is. Magic is simply used in place of what would normally be machines. The handiwork consists of largely the same makeup."

Ferromancy

Nimbats within Flight Progression’s Metallurgy sect use magic to assist in forging basic metals and alloys to assist in their work.
“Before Production Flight became inaugurated into Colony infrastructure, ferromancy was considered a supplementary art. A branch of geomancy that was often left in obscurity, for the world of metal was considered a herald of devastating change. So far, it seems that the most change to come of the Colony seems to be in structural integrity improvement and whatever new toy Progression has come up with.”

Chronomancy

The altering of the flow of time in extremely localized spaces and mediums. For example, Nimbats in Flights Production and Preservation can use this school of magic to speed up plant germination, while medical personnel can use it to place gravely-injured individuals into a safe stasis.
"If a Nimbat ever actually tells you that time is not on your side, pray that you take it to heart.”

Spatiomancy

The ability to create portals and “hammer spaces”. Flight Preservation tends to use this magic to create “larger on the inside'' spaces to allow mass storage of material.
“As useful as the control of space is, it has also led to one of their greatest adversaries: containment. I have taken up my Advocate’s advice and kept around a small leather bag on my person. The citizens take the threat of a potential Nimbag of Holding very, very seriously.”

Bodily Manipulation

Runic Enchantment

Imbuing a rune on one’s person, to change attributes about them. Most Flights use various types of runes, which have been observed to produce a variety of effects, on both animate and inanimate objects. This is further detailed below, in another section.
"In one example of some of the creativity prescribed in runes, one Nimbat described how they had imbued a chest with a rune of their own making, granting it the power to make it burrow itself into the dirt if unwanted paws were to touch it. The question is, would it not be so obvious that an enchantment was present if some sort of estranged marking was on it?”

Transformation

A handful of Nimbats aligned with Flight Proliferation offer an optional ritual to Visitants that wish to integrate into the Colony at the highest peak, by altering their body into a fitting design for a Nimbat.
"Most reported cases have apparently been temporary or tied with conditions. There is a tale or two regarding particularly fiendish sorts turning the unaware into one of their own ken. There have actually been treatments provided for such a thing. However, at the highest level of governing, a more permanent instance can be requested. With a lot of paperwork, of course.”

Regeneration

Healing magic. Many Nimbats know how to heal people, but only to a basic degree. The most skilled users are part of Flight Populace, and act as makeshift medics to tend to injured, be it a fully-fledged member of the Colony, or a Visitant. This healing magic involves amplifying the body’s natural healing processes, essentially cutting down the time for injuries to produce healthy tissues.
"The magic is very potent, usually healing nearly any sort of malady in the span of an afternoon. Members of Proliferation Flight often learn this magic, as they are often working as emergency medical technicians."

Miscellaneous

Illusory Spells

Many Nimbats of Flight Protection are illusionists, given that they desire to maintain a level of docility to their society. A large network of them maintain the large illusion field around the Colony, preventing it from being witnessed by outsiders. Some Nimbats take their illusion-craft further, producing doubles, tricks of light, and false stimuli.
"This is notably different from Psionic Communication, due to the fact that the affected conscience is still very much in the real world. Interestingly, illusory spells are often treated with a special kind of scrutiny. Not merely for their tendency to be used in pranks, but rather for the lack of actual development. Local pranksters are derided with frustration, often being tied to the more benevolent nature behind Eislyn’s illusory spells.”

Runic Enchantment
Within Runic Enchantment, there is a wide range of possibilities, ranging from bodily alteration to imbuing items with magical properties.

Corporeal Enchantments

Strength

Specific enchantments grant the user to incorporate increased physical strength. Lifting, pulling, pushing, and other forms of physical exertion are effectively amplified through the magically-augmented strength of the imbued subject.
“One interesting piece of cultural exchange on the meaning and accessibility of achieving bodily strength happened from a single happenstance moment—I could not hold back laughter as a two-foot tall Nimbat lifted an entire tree over their head and politely asked his mother where to put it.”

Sensory Improvements

When strength and power aren’t exactly what is most needed, Nimbats are able to bestow improved sensory abilities. For example, improved eyesight, smell, or hearing.
. “If the world beyond the Colony were to find a cure for blindness, what would happen to prescription lenses? Would business run out? Would the cure cost an arm and a leg? Perhaps using the Colony as a simulation for treatments on individuals with eyesight problems could provide some meaningful insight.”

Imbued Enchantments

Durability

As it would imply, the enchantment is often applied to tools to prevent them from dulling or degrading.
“The Colony will surely never understand The Ship of Theseus. They have overreached it.”

Soulbinding

Some Nimbats, when they wish not to lose a certain item, have it soulbound to them. They, from then on, are able to locate the item without issue, even if it is obscured or in another location.
“‘Become a Nimbat today, and you will never lose your car keys again!’ No, to be frank, I think I would still find a way to lose them even after soulbinding to them.”

Rebounding

An item imbued with this enchantment can be recalled to the bound person at will. For example, if a Nimbat throws a dart, they are able to recall it to their hand after it hits the board. This recalling can either be a “manifestation” in their hand, or it could physically return to them, levitating through the air.
“One must imagine the importance and integrity of mental health care and data-logged weaponry under soulbindery. Marshall can be carrying absolutely nothing on her person, but if I so far as look at her funny, I could have three greatswords heading for my neck in a matter of seconds.”

Flight-Specific Enchantments

Fertilization

For Flight Preservation, enchanters are able to imbue the land itself with fertility, increasing crop growth and yield.
“It is honestly incredibly interesting to see this magic at work. Priest-like enchanter Nimbats within Flight Preservation make pilgrimages to the farmlands, regularly booning the very soil. They are overseen by Kara, the Agriculture sect leader of Flight Preservation, and she tends to take this obligation incredibly seriously. They report her extensive vetting of enchanter applications, making sure they will attend to the duty as seriously as possible.”
